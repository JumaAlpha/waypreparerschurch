Our Overseer

Philip Ndunda

Born and raised in Embu County, Philip Ndunda began his spiritual journey at 19. Today, he inspires lives worldwide with his commitment to spreading the Word of God.

He is a Father, Mentor, and the publisher of Prophets God's Agent of Change and Dynamics of Prayer.

His journey was shaped by mentors like Bishop Peter Mankura and Bishop Samuel Mulumbi, and he continues to carry their wisdom forward in his ministry.

A Word from Pastor Esther Ndunda

"As we continue this journey of faith together, my prayer is that every person who steps into Way Preparer's Church experiences the transformative power of God's love. We are called to be a beacon of hope, love, and unity, and I believe that each of us has a vital role to play in this mission."

Pastor Esther Ndunda has been a cornerstone of support in the church's vision, working tirelessly to uplift and empower the congregation through her teachings and mentorship. Her dedication to women's ministry and her unwavering faith have been a source of inspiration for many.

Our Church

Way Preparer's Church is an Independent Charismatic, non-denominational Church founded on the Rock, Jesus Christ. It was launched on 12th February 2005 by Visionaries Overseer Philip Ndunda and his wife, Pastor Esther Ndunda, in obedience to God's call and directions.
