Order of Services

Explore our Sunday Worship Services schedule and details.

First Service - 8:00 AM

First Service - 8:00 AM

  • Prayer: Begin the day by inviting God's presence into the service.
  • Praise and Worship: Lift hearts in praise with music glorifying God's name.
  • Scripture Reading: A Bible passage is read to inspire and encourage the congregation.
  • Sermon: The pastor shares the Word of God, teaching and guiding us in faith.
  • Offering: Present tithes and gifts as an act of worship and stewardship.
  • Closing Prayer: Conclude by thanking God and asking blessings for the week ahead.

Second Service - 10:30 AM

Second Service - 10:30 AM

  • Prayer: We welcome God's presence into the service.
  • Praise and Worship: Songs of praise are sung to glorify God's name.
  • Scripture Reading: A Bible passage is read to uplift the congregation.
  • Sermon: The pastor shares God's teachings to guide us in our spiritual walk.
  • Offering: Tithes and offerings are presented as an act of worship.
  • Closing Prayer: We conclude by thanking God for His blessings.

Youth Service - 12:00 PM

Youth Service - 12:00 PM

  • Prayer: Begin with a time of prayer and worship.
  • Praise and Worship: Youth-led songs glorifying God.
  • Scripture Reading: Bible reading relevant to youth challenges and growth.
  • Sermon: A message addressing youth needs and spiritual development.
  • Offering: Youth contribute tithes and gifts in worship.
  • Closing Prayer: Blessing and guidance for the youth in their daily lives.
